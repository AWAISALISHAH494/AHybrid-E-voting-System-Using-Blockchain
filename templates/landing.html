Why HybridVote?

Built with security, transparency, and performance in mind.

AES-256 Encryption

Every vote is encrypted with military-grade AES-256-CBC encryption before storage, ensuring complete vote confidentiality.

Blockchain Verified

Final results are hashed and stored on an Ethereum blockchain, making them impossible to tamper with or manipulate.

Hybrid Voting

Supports both electronic voting and manual ballot entry, combining the best of digital and traditional voting systems.

Duplicate Prevention

Advanced cryptographic hashing prevents any voter from casting multiple votes while maintaining complete anonymity.

Real-time Results

Watch results unfold in real-time with beautiful charts and detailed analytics on the admin dashboard.

Audit Trail

Complete transparency with verifiable audit trails. Anyone can verify election integrity through blockchain records.

How It Works

Three simple steps to a tamper-proof election.

1

Register & Authenticate

Register with your CNIC and create a secure account. Your identity is verified to ensure only eligible voters participate.

2

Cast Your Vote

Select your candidate and submit. Your vote is instantly encrypted with AES-256 and stored securely in the cloud database.

3

Blockchain Seal

Once voting ends, all results are combined, hashed with SHA-256, and permanently sealed on the blockchain network.
