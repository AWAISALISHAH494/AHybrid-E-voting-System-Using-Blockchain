{{ icons.icon_info() }} How Vote Verification Works

1. When you cast your vote, it is encrypted with AES-256-CBC and a unique receipt code is generated.

2. The receipt code is linked to your encrypted vote but does not reveal your identity.

3. When you verify, the system decrypts your vote using the same encryption key and shows which candidate it was cast for.

4. If decryption succeeds and the candidate is valid, your vote is intact and untampered.
